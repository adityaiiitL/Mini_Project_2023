    Precision Farming and Predictive Analysis

    We specialize in precision farming and predictive analysis of crop yield, using advanced technologies to optimize production and minimize waste for a more sustainable future of farming.

    Make predictions and Enhance Crop Yield

    We provide you with some very amazing features of the application that allow us to predict the best suitable crop for our farm, recommends fertilizers for them and then resulting crop yield.

    Features

    Crop Recommendation

    Land over this feature where you can get the best fit Crop suitable for your agricultural land.

    Fertilizers Recommendation

    You can get to know which fertilizers types are best for your current crop and then buy them from our portal.

    Crop Yield Prediction

    We provide you the feature to predict the estimated Crop Yield value for your farm.

    How it Works

    • Crop Recommendation

      • - Using various ML technologies, we find the best fit crop suitable for your agricultural field.

      • - The prediction depends on the parameters such as soil and weather conditions.

    • Fertilizers Recommendation

      • - Users are provided the feature to determine which fertilizers are best for their crop growth.

      • - Following graph shows the feature dependency of the final fertiliser label, that is, which factors the fertilizer selection, the most.



    • Crop Yield Prediction

      • - Using advanced Machine Learning technologies and research works, we have created a prediction system that estimates the Crop Yield for a specific Crop type.

      • - User has to specify the required soil and weather paramters depending on which the Machine Learning Regressor Model esitmates the Crop Yield.

      • - This plot shows the analysis of crops versus final Crop Yield estimation depending on the parameters specified by the user.
